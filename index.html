Motivation

It has been pretty demotivating to do most daily tasks in 2020, so as first year university students, our motivation for this project was to try and stay as healthy and as fit as possible during lockdown. There are plenty of apps out there that encourage users to keep fit and track their progress, but the amount of apps that really excel in motivating users to actually exercise and stick to a healthy diet daily are quite limited.

We "HEHEXD" aimed to overcome this problem with our project app called "Power Up", which is an android application that allows users to track their daily calorie intake and gives rewards for achieving certain goals each month. It has the ability to share results on weight, steps, meals consumed, etc, to others through social media and other users of "Power Up". The "Power Up" app can provide detailed graphs and statistics on users' fitness results and can provide them with personalised recipes and motivational videos based on their daily or monthly goals.

Aim and Goals

Project Aim: To create a fitness app that allows people to stay fit and healthy amidst this pandemic, and even after our lives are back to normal.

Listed below are 3 goals that we believe will help us to achieve our aim of allowing people to build up their fitness and health during the current lockdown and beyond.


  1. Calorie Count Tracking: In order to achieve the aim of our project, a great way to keep people healthy and on top of their fitness, is by implementing certain features such as calorie counter that lets users search through a database of meals and add the meals they've consumed throughout the day to a journal. This calorie counter will calculate the calories in each meal they have consumed based on portion sizes. This goal is to about letting consumers track their diet so that they feel energised and as fit as possible.

  2. Recipe/Meal Suggestions: While tracking calories is the main feature of our project, another goal we have to help achieve our aim is to give consumers recipe and meal suggestions based on their calorie count and meals consumed. If a user has certain tastes and prefers to eat certain meals over others, our app will make suggestions on what other types of healthy meals they would enjoy and how to cook them with detailed recipes. This goal is all about encouraging users to try new healthy foods that will drastically improve their diet if they happen to be consuming meals that aren't necessarily considered 'healthy'.

  3. Rewards System: Because our aim is all about encouraging people to stay fit and energetic during this unfortunate pandemic, our third goal is to provide users with rewards for achieving certain milestones such as 10,000 steps taken in one day, or lost 2kg in a week, etc. Once a user achieves a milestone, they will be rewarded with fitness points that can be exchanged for discounts and goodies from third party fitness websites and businesses.


Scope

Project Title: POWER UP Melbourne


Project Requirements:

  1. The app should contain all the five Minimum Viable Features (Calorie Counter, Goal Setting, Social Sharing, Recipe/ Meal suggestions and Motivational videos)

    2. Calorie Counter: This feature of our application will work by the user inputting the name of the item into a search bar, once entered the area below will update with any relevant items. If the user then presses on the item in the area below a more detailed description showing all the nutritional facts about that item will appear, the user will be able to change the amount (weight) and the nutritional values should adjust accordingly. The user can add that item to the tracker from within the detailed description or from the main screen.

    Goal Setting:This feature of our application will let the user save goals that suit them. We will give each goal a level system. As we all know not all people that exercise every day, therefore they must start from the beginning of getting fit or healthy. And Lev 1 goals will help the users to achieve their very first beginner goal by letting the user get used to the Tasks or getting used to the habit of exercising. And it will stop at lev 3. Lev 3 is where the body builder’s goals, and suggestion will be made to alert user this is from some professional bodybuilders’ goal. Each Lev of goals will help different users to achieve their own suitable meaning of being healthy.

    Social Sharing:This feature of our application allows users to share their progress and results with their friends and family over other social media applications such as Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, etc. They can send other people a small report of their achievements over any period of time that they choose, e.g. 2 days, 2 weeks, 1 month etc. With this feature, users can also send their progress to other users of our application and compare their results side by side with their friends if they use our application. This feature will be fairly easy to implement as it simply involves grabbing the user's inputs of their progress and printing it into a nice template for sharing to other social media applications, such as Messenger, Instagram, Facebook, etc. If the user does not have any social media applications, they can also share this information in an email format, as if they were simply sharing a YouTube video or website.

    Recipe/Meal Suggestion:This feature of our application has the ability to give the user meal suggestions. The app will recommend ingredients/meal alternatives based on the past meal history and the type of exercise or goals needed to be achieved. The app will also allow you to pick custom meal so users will not be limited to recommended options.

    Motivational Videos: This feature of the application will show the user motivational videos which are to keep users motivated to do some fitness or exercise.

  2. The app should be completed and presented by the 21st of October 2020

  3. App must be user friendly for people of ages 12+

    4. This app can be used by kids and teens aged 12-18 under parental guidance as the it contains in-app purchases.

  4. App should cater for people with special needs

    5. The app contains larger text to help people with special need and people of older age read. It also contains contrasting colour modes such as dark mode which reduces the strain on the eye.
